## 3. IDEA OWNERSHIP & IP PROTECTION

### Ownership

**Creator Retains:**
- **100% ownership** of idea
- **Fikra Valley has:** No ownership stake

**License to Platform:**
- **What:** Right to display, analyze, match with mentors
- **Duration:** While account is active
- **Revocable:** Yes
- **Exclusive:** No

**If Idea Deleted:**
- Removed from public view within 24 hours
- Kept in archive for legal/fraud prevention only
- Duration: 90 days then permanent deletion
- Cannot be restored after 90 days

### Protection Mechanisms

**Visibility Controls:**

Levels:
- **Private:** Only you see it
- **Team:** You + invited team members
- **Mentors:** You + matched mentors only
- **Community:** Your sector community only
- **Public:** Everyone (for funding applications)

Default: **Private**  
User Controlled: **Yes**

**Watermarking:**
- **Enabled:** Yes
- **Method:** Invisible digital fingerprint on all documents
- **Purpose:** Prove ownership if dispute arises

**NDA for Mentors:**
- **Required:** Yes
- **Terms:** Cannot share idea details with third parties
- **Enforcement:** Violation = removal from platform

**Idea Registry:**
- **What:** Timestamped proof of idea submission
- **Blockchain:** No (too expensive, centralized DB sufficient)
- **Certificate:** PDF certificate with timestamp + hash
- **Legal Value:** Prima facie evidence of prior art

### What We Do NOT Protect

- **Patent Rights:** We don't file patents for you
- **Trademark:** You must register yourself
- **Copyright:** Automatic, but you can register

**Recommendations:**
- Consult IP lawyer for high-value innovations
- File trademark if brand-dependent
- Consider patent for true technical innovations

**Partners:**
- **OMPIC:** Moroccan IP office - discounted services
- **IP Lawyers:** Pre-vetted, startup-friendly rates

## 4. RECEIPT VALIDATION POLICIES

### Valid Receipts

**Amount:**
- **Exact:** 3.00 DH
- **Tolerance:** 0 DH (must be exact)

**Date:**
- **Max Age:** 90 days
- **Reason:** Recent validation > old validation

**Format:**

Accepted:
- Handwritten receipt (signed + dated)
- Mobile money screenshot (Orange Money, Barid Cash, etc.)
- Digital signature (signed PDF)
- Email confirmation (if contains payment proof)

Required Elements:
- **Amount:** Clearly visible: 3 DH
- **Date:** DD/MM/YYYY format
- **Signature or Confirmation:** Name or digital proof
- **Purpose:** Optional but helpful: "Validation [idea name]"

**Validation Process:**
1. **OCR:** Automatic extraction
2. **Fraud Check:** Duplicate detection
3. **Human Review:** If confidence < 80%
4. **Approval:** Auto or manual

### Invalid Receipts

‚ùå Wrong amount (not 3 DH)  
‚ùå Too old (> 90 days)  
‚ùå Duplicate (same receipt uploaded twice)  
‚ùå Forged (Photoshopped, fake signature)  
‚ùå Self-signed (creator paid themselves)  
‚ùå Family only (> 50% from family members)  
‚ùå Blurry (unreadable)  
‚ùå Incomplete (missing date or signature)  

### Fraud Detection

**Duplicate Images:**
- **Method:** Perceptual hashing
- **Tolerance:** 98% similarity = flag

**Suspicious Patterns:**
- All receipts same day (> 20 on same day)
- All receipts same handwriting (> 30 identical)
- All receipts same location (GPS metadata shows same device)
- Rapid submission (> 50 in 1 hour)

**Consequences:**
- **First Offense:** Warning + receipts removed
- **Second Offense:** 3-day suspension
- **Third Offense:** Permanent ban + all ideas removed

**Appeals:**
- **Allowed:** Yes
- **Process:** Submit evidence + explanation
- **Review Time:** 48 hours
- **Independent Reviewer:** Yes

### Gamification Concern

**Problem:** Users might game receipts

**Mitigations:**
- Human review threshold: Flag if > 100 receipts
- Velocity checks: Flag if 50+ receipts in 24 hours
- Quality over quantity: 10 receipts with rich context > 100 generic
- Intimacy score required: Must have lived experience (Locke)
- Spot audits: Random 5% sample checked thoroughly

## 5. SCORING & QUALIFICATION POLICIES

### Transparency

**Algorithm Published:** Yes  
**Location:** https://fikravalley.ma/scoring-methodology

**User Sees:**
- Current score (real-time)
- Score breakdown (Clarity, Decision, Intimacy)
- Gaps (exactly what's missing)
- Potential score (if gaps filled)

**User Does NOT See:**
- Other users' scores (private)
- Ranking (no public leaderboard)
- Reviewer identities (anonymous)

### Objectivity

**Automated Scoring:** 70% of total score  
**Human Review:** 30% (clarity, feasibility subjective parts)

**Bias Prevention:**
- Remove names (reviewers don't see creator name)
- Remove location (don't see city to avoid urban bias)
- Remove gender markers (neutral presentation)
- Diverse reviewer pool (mix of backgrounds, sectors)

**Calibration:**
- **Frequency:** Weekly
- **Method:** Random 10% double-reviewed
- **Consistency Target:** > 90% inter-rater reliability
