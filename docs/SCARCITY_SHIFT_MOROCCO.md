## üíé New Scarcities Emerging in Morocco

### 1. **Local Context & Trust** (Most Important)

**What it is:** Deep understanding of Moroccan market, culture, relationships, trust networks.

**Why it's scarce:**
- AI can't replicate **local relationships** (family, neighborhood, community)
- AI can't build **trust** (requires human connection, reputation, word-of-mouth)
- AI can't navigate **Moroccan business culture** (Darija nuances, social dynamics)

**Examples:**
- **Food Delivery:** AI can optimize routes, but **trust** comes from "driver is your neighbor"
- **FinTech:** AI can process payments, but **trust** comes from "my cousin uses this"
- **EdTech:** AI can generate content, but **trust** comes from "teacher from my city"

**Value Migration:**
- Premium moves from **generic expertise** ‚Üí **local trust & relationships**

---

### 2. **Execution & Coordination** (Second Most Important)

**What it is:** Actually building, delivering, coordinating in Morocco's infrastructure reality.

**Why it's scarce:**
- AI can't **deliver food** in Casablanca traffic
- AI can't **navigate 2G connectivity** issues
- AI can't **coordinate cash + mobile money** payments
- AI can't **handle PDPL compliance** in practice
- AI can't **manage diaspora relationships** (remittances, family connections)

**Examples:**
- **Food Delivery:** AI optimizes, but **execution** = driver navigating Casablanca + cash handling
- **EdTech:** AI generates content, but **execution** = offline delivery + mobile money integration
- **FinTech:** AI processes transactions, but **execution** = M-Wallet integration + PDPL compliance

**Value Migration:**
- Premium moves from **strategy/analysis** ‚Üí **execution/coordination**
