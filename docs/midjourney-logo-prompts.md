## Style Parameters

### Recommended Settings:
- `--style raw` - For cleaner, more controlled results
- `--ar 1:1` - Square format for logo
- `--v 6` or latest - For best quality
- `--stylize 100-250` - Adjust for more/less creative interpretation

### Color Palette Reference:
- **Primary**: Indigo/Purple (#4f46e5, #6366f1)
- **Accents**: Orange (#f97316), Yellow (#eab308), Green (#22c55e), Light Blue (#3b82f6)
- **Background**: White or transparent

### Variations to Try:
1. Add `--no text` to avoid text in logo
2. Add `--seed [number]` to maintain consistency
3. Use `--chaos 0-50` for more variation
4. Try `--weird 0-1000` for unique interpretations
5. Always include `--no Persian Iranian Middle Eastern` to ensure authentic Moroccan representation
6. Add `North African Berber` or `Maghrebi` to emphasize regional authenticity

## Authentic Moroccan Cultural Elements

### Traditional Clothing:
- **Djellaba**: Long, loose-fitting robe with hood, typically in neutral colors (beige, brown, white, grey)
- **Tarboosh/Fez**: Traditional red felt hat with tassel (optional, more formal)
- **Headscarf/Turban**: Traditional head wrap (alternative to tarboosh)

### Physical Features:
- **North African Berber features**: Distinct from Middle Eastern/Persian features
- **Maghrebi characteristics**: Reflects the diverse ethnic makeup of Morocco

### Cultural Context:
- **Mint tea ceremony**: Central to Moroccan hospitality and social interaction
- **Traditional tea pot**: Distinctive Moroccan design with curved spout
- **Tea cup and saucer**: Traditional Moroccan tea set design

### What to Avoid:
- ❌ Persian/Iranian clothing (different traditional dress)
- ❌ Middle Eastern turbans (different style from Moroccan)
- ❌ Generic "Arab" features (Morocco has distinct North African characteristics)
- ❌ Non-Moroccan tea ceremony elements

### Keywords for Authenticity:
- "North African Berber"
- "Maghrebi"
- "Moroccan djellaba"
- "Moroccan tarboosh"
- "Atlas Mountains" (geographic reference)
- "Maghreb region"
