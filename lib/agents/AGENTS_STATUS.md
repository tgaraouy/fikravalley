## ğŸ“ Locke Philosophy Integration

Both completed agents deeply integrate John Locke's philosophy:

### FIKRA (Idea Clarifier)
- **Intimacy Scoring**: Measures "true knowing" vs "knowing of"
- **Personal Experience**: Prioritizes lived experience
- **Thinking Prompts**: Encourages reflection (like Locke's pencil marks)
- **Margin Notes**: Tracks user's thinking evolution

### PROOF (Evidence Collector)
- **3-DH Receipt = Pencil Mark**: Physical proof of engagement
- **Conversation as Education**: Each receipt = learning moment
- **Intimacy Through Action**: 200 conversations = deep knowledge
- **Transformation Journey**: From "knowing of" to "true knowing"

**Core Quote:**
> "Reading furnishes the mind with materials of knowledge.  
> It is thinking makes what we read ours."  
> - John Locke

**Applied:**
- **Reading** = Hearing about a problem
- **Thinking** = Clarifying through questions (FIKRA)
- **Making it ours** = Proving through action (PROOF)
